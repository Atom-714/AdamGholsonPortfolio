Prototypes

This section highlights a collection of gameplay prototypes that focus on unique mechanics, systems, and experiments. While not full games, these projects showcase my ability to design and implement complex features, explore new ideas, and apply what I’ve learned in creative ways.

April, 2025

Warlords Trading Card Game

A digital prototype of my original trading card game, Warlords, developed for my AI class. This project features a functional Unity implementation of the game’s mechanics along with an AI opponent powered by a Minimax algorithm, enabling it to make strategic decisions about which resources to sacrifice and which cards to play. The prototype demonstrates both game design and artificial intelligence integration in gameplay systems.

December, 2024

DnD Battle System Prototype

A turn-based RPG prototype inspired by Dungeons & Dragons, created for my Game Development 2D class. The project replicates DnD-style combat, including abilities, spells, and turn-based mechanics. I focused on designing a modular move system using interfaces, parent classes, and scriptable objects, allowing effects and abilities to be easily customized, reused, and expanded for future development.
